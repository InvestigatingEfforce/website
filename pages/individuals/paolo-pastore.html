Paolo Pastore became Managing Director of Efforce in January 2022. Over the following two years, he became the single most revealing insider source in the entire Efforce investigation — privately admitting that WOZX had been “pumped and dumped by private investors,” confirming wallet sales to a U.S. venture capital firm, exposing the SEELE/Ponzi connection, describing Castiglione as having “told a lot of lies,” and confirming Vanetti’s total control over the crypto keys.

Then, allegedly under internal pressure, Pastore turned hostile — ceasing cooperation and aligning with the same entities whose misconduct he had documented.

Pastore’s private statements are devastating because they come from inside the operation. He is not an outside observer guessing at wrongdoing — he is the Managing Director confirming it.

Alleged Criminal Charges

1. Alleged Securities Fraud (SEC Rule 10b-5)

As Managing Director with knowledge of the pump-and-dump scheme, Pastore allegedly failed to disclose material information to token holders:

  • “Pumped and dumped by private investors” (Nov 15, 2022) — an admission that the core investment narrative was fraudulent, made privately while investors continued to hold.
  • Wallet 3 (0x697D456D…) — confirmed sold to a U.S. venture capital firm, containing 75M WOZX converted to $49M in alternative cryptocurrencies (confirmed Feb 18, 2025).
  • Non-disclosure of MPI’s $283M profits — as MD, Pastore knew the “long-term institutional investors” had dumped 186.6M WOZX for massive profits while retail lost everything.

Applicable laws: U.S. 18 U.S.C. § 1343 (wire fraud, up to 20 years), SEC Rule 10b-5 (material omissions in connection with securities).

2. Alleged Market Manipulation

Pastore possessed insider knowledge of the dump mechanism and failed to act:

  • Wallet 3 sold to U.S. VC (Nov 15, 2022) — confirmed awareness that treasury tokens were being sold to external parties without disclosure.
  • 160M WOZX unaccounted from ETW — as MD, bore responsibility for explaining where treasury funds went. No explanation was ever provided.

Applicable laws: SEC Rule 10b-5, EU MAR Art. 12.

3. Alleged Aiding and Abetting

By privately acknowledging fraud while publicly maintaining his role as Managing Director, Pastore allegedly provided ongoing legitimacy to the operation. His continued presence signalled to investors that Efforce was a functioning, professionally managed company — contradicting what he privately admitted.

Alleged SEC Liability (U.S.)

Pastore’s private confirmations of U.S. connections create direct SEC jurisdiction:

  • Wallet 3 sold to U.S. VC — direct U.S. involvement in token treasury distribution.

Key Evidence — The Three Bombshells

Bombshell #1: The Pump-and-Dump Admission

“Pumped and dumped by private investors.”

— Paolo Pastore, private communication, Nov 15, 2022

Why this matters: This is a serving Managing Director confirming, in his own words, that WOZX was the subject of a pump-and-dump scheme. This single statement potentially transforms the entire case from one of “failed promises” to one of deliberate, known fraud. At the time of this admission, retail investors were still holding WOZX based on the promise that private investors were “long-term institutional supporters.”

Legal significance: Under SEC Rule 10b-5, a corporate officer who knows a security has been manipulated and fails to disclose this to the market commits a material omission. Under U.S. wire fraud statute (18 U.S.C. § 1343), knowledge of a scheme to defraud through electronic communications carries up to 20 years imprisonment.

Bombshell #2: The SEELE Connection Exposed

“Andrea was the one pushing the SEELE relationship while denying it publicly.”

— Paolo Pastore, private communication, Dec 11, 2023

Why this matters: SEELE/YuanYi is a Chinese Ponzi organisation (“传销” pyramid scheme) currently under active police investigation in Dongguan, China. It used WOZX as its flagship investment product, with victims losing approximately $200K+. Pastore confirmed that this relationship was actively driven by a co-founder — not a coincidental association but a deliberate business strategy.

Legal significance: Knowingly facilitating a Ponzi scheme’s access to a token is potentially criminal under multiple jurisdictions. Pastore’s revelation, combined with GDAC 2020 attendance records and video call evidence, creates a chain of evidence linking Efforce leadership directly to SEELE’s operations.

Bombshell #3: The $49M Alt-Coin Wallet
