Andrea Castiglione was the most visible and active representative of Efforce from 2020 to 2023. He repeatedly assured token holders that private investors were “long-term cornerstone institutions” with “NONE OF THEM HAS THE INTENTION TO SELL”, that no team tokens had been moved “in year 1”, that AitherCO2 was a financially robust “mother company” with $250–450M revenue, and that a revolutionary platform was “days/weeks/months” away. Every single one of these core assurances was proven false.

The private investors dumped over $300 million. $68 million+ in treasury tokens vanished without explanation. AitherCO2 was insolvent with a €319.9 million tax liability that was never disclosed. The platform never launched in any functional form.

Alleged Criminal Charges

1. Fraud by False Representation

Castiglione repeatedly made statements he knew or should have known were false to induce investment and continued holding of WOZX.

  • “NONE OF THEM HAS THE INTENTION TO SELL” (Dec 2, 2020) — MPI dumped $283M+.
  • “We haven’t moved any of our tokens in year 1” (May 25, 2021) — $68M+ already gone.
  • Repeated claims AitherCO2 had “$250–450M revenue” while it faced a €319.9M tax bomb.
  • Over 78 separate assurances of imminent platform launches, staking, and Tier-1 listings — none delivered.

Laws: Malta Criminal Code Art. 308, Italian Penal Code Art. 640, U.S. 18 U.S.C. § 1343.

2. Alleged Market Manipulation

Castiglione’s assurances created artificial confidence while MPI and treasury wallets dumped hundreds of millions.

3. Alleged Aiding and Abetting Fraud / Money Laundering

Castiglione continued to promote Efforce while aware (or recklessly unaware) of SeeleN/GDAC ties, the €319.9M AitherCO2 liability, and the nominal €510 stake transfer.
