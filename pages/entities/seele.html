SEELE / YuanYi (元一)

Alleged WOZX Involvement in Chinese Ponzi Scheme (2019–2025)

Between 2019 and 2023, the Chinese Ponzi organisation “SEELE Ecology” (also known as “YuanYi / 元一”) used WOZX as one of its flagship investment products. The scheme is under active criminal investigation by Chinese police as a pyramid-selling (传销) case involving hundreds of billions of RMB across multiple tokens.

Rebuttals From Efforce

“Andrea was the one pushing the SEELE relationship while denying it publicly.”

— Paolo Pastore (Efforce Managing Director), private message, 11 December 2023

Whistleblower Evidence

A protected Chinese whistleblower who lost ~$200,000 USD in WOZX has provided conclusive evidence that:

  • SEELE leader Wang Peng personally promoted WOZX private placement with $10+ price targets.
  • Efforce co-founder Andrea Castiglione gave exclusive speeches to SEELE members (GDAC 2020 Seoul + multiple video calls in 2022–2023).
  • Efforce representatives knew WOZX was being sold via an alleged Ponzi structure but continued the relationship and shielded Andrea Castiglione’s continued involvement from their official channels.
  • Chinese media named WOZX in the SEELE fraud ecosystem as early as February 2021.

Andrea Castiglione’s Public Denials vs Evidence

Andrea Castiglione publicly denied any partnership with SEELE/GDAC. Despite these public denials, he personally attended SEELE-organised events and gave private talks to their members as late as 2023.

Andrea Castiglione Publicly Confirmed Most Private Investors Were Chinese

  • 9 Dec 2020: “A large portion of our private sale investors are from China... PIs were chosen as large buyers with existing ecosystems committed to long-term support.”
  • 10 Feb 2021: “Most Private sale investors are (A) Institutionals B) In Asia and C) Vested for long-term success of company.”
  • 21 Feb 2021: “All the investors have a long-term interest... because of their institutional role and sectors of operations.”

Jacopo Visetti (Majority Owner): Contradicting the Asia Connection

“I never understood the connection to Asia etc, the private investors were US based etc.”
— Jacopo Visetti, private Discord call, July 2024

GDAC 2020 Seoul — Co-organised by SEELE-N

Participating / co-organising organisations included: GDAC • Hainan Digital Transaction Research Institute • Legen • Efforce • BCH Ecological Fund • Polychain Capital • Huobi • Digital Cash • Anxin Charity Foundation

Huobi → HBTC → BCH Ecological Fund Connection

Efforce signed a high-profile “strategic partnership” with Huobi in August 2020. Yet WOZX was launched on HBTC — founded by Huobi’s ex-CTO, financed by Huobi, and using Huobi technology — instead of Huobi itself.

HBTC was later accused of laundering hundreds of millions in stolen crypto (Forbes, June 2020) and shut down in 2021, denying token holders access to transaction history.

The same BCH Ecological Fund that co-organised GDAC 2020 was later linked to a £76M UK-registered crypto Ponzi (Protos, 2021).

Summary

WOZX was promoted as a flagship product within the SEELE/YuanYi pyramid scheme, with evidence of direct involvement by Efforce co-founder Andrea Castiglione — despite his public denials. The scheme is under active Chinese police investigation with multiple arrests. Efforce’s own public statements confirmed Chinese/Asian private investors, while majority owner Visetti claimed ignorance of the Asia connection. The Pastore revelation that “Andrea was the one pushing the SEELE relationship while denying it publicly” remains unrebutted.
