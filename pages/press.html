The Story

Apple co-founder Steve Wozniak lent his name and reputation to Efforce, a blockchain energy efficiency platform that launched the WOZX token in December 2020. On-chain evidence, corporate filings, and insider communications suggest the project may have been used to facilitate one of the largest alleged pump-and-dump schemes in crypto history.

Private investors acquired 450M WOZX tokens for approximately $0.10 each and realised an estimated $336M in profits. Retail investors who purchased at market prices have suffered losses exceeding $400M. The company that issued the token — Efforce Ltd (Malta) — filed accounts showing zero employees, zero staff costs, and €25K total revenue across four years. Its claimed parent company, AitherCO2, carried a concealed €319.9M tax liability. A California class action (25CV459519) is now pending. Formal complaints have been filed with regulators in five jurisdictions.

Individual

Steve Wozniak

11% shareholder · Named co-founder · Apple co-founder

NO REBUTTAL RECEIVED

Wozniak was marketed as co-founder and engineer of Efforce from its public launch, despite not receiving shares until August 2019 — 14 months after incorporation. He told Yahoo Finance in December 2020 that he saw WOZX tokens as “shares of stock,” potentially satisfying the Howey Test for securities classification. Despite whitepaper exclusions for U.S. persons, WOZX was promoted in Times Square and via major U.S. media. His own Managing Director, Paolo Pastore, later said he “abandoned ship without any kind of direction.” He was notified of the investigation via 20 emails (some through his associate Seamus) and via Hogan Lovells. No substantive response was received. He is a named defendant in case 25CV459519.

KEY EVIDENCE:
  • Memorandum of Association (Jun 2018) vs. share transfer (Aug 2019) — 14-month gap
  • Yahoo Finance “shares of stock” interview (Dec 8, 2020)
  • Times Square billboard & U.S.-targeted marketing despite whitepaper exclusion
  • 20 outreach emails (2023–2025) — no substantive response; blocked after Cameo reply
  • Pastore admission: “abandoned ship” and “owners are to blame 100%, Woz as well”

LEGAL THREATS ONLY
