Blockchain
The $336M Token Dump
Private investors bought 450M WOZX at $0.10 and realised ~$336M. HTX Ventures (fmr Huobi) identified as lead buyer per ICODrops. On Oct 29, 2021, 186.6M tokens were dumped in a single day. The CEO said: “Nothing illegal.”

The Missing $68 Million
Efforce’s 550M treasury wallet transferred 173.3M WOZX to 15 recipient wallets. 92.4% is unaccounted for. No crypto transactions appear in any company filing across four jurisdictions. Wallet key holders identified: Carloni, Hardesty, Vanetti.
