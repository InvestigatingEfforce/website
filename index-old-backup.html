UK Defamation Act 2013 s.4 – Public Interest Publication
This website publishes claims made in good faith and in the public interest concerning Efforce Ltd, the WOZX token, and related parties — subjects of ongoing U.S. litigation (Superior Court of California, Santa Clara County, Case No. 25CV459519: Benner & Copley v. Wozniak, Efforce Ltd et al. — alleging fraud, misrepresentation, and related claims). All statements are alleged and based on verifiable evidence (court filings, blockchain records, official documents). Notice was served on 3 May 2025 to Hogan Lovells.
